Our Story

Over 40 years of excellence, multiculturalism, and educational commitment in Seychelles.

A Legacy of Learning

Founded in 1983, the Ecole Française des Seychelles proudly celebrated its 40th anniversary in 2023. Managed by a dedicated parents' association, we have been committed since our inception to the development and influence of the establishment, working closely with the management team and institutional partners.

EFS is a school under convention with the AEFE and approved by the French Ministry of National Education. We welcome students from preschool to high school in a truly international and multicultural environment.
