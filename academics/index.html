High School: The CNED Model

Our high school students (1ère and Terminale) benefit from a unique, highly structured distance-learning integration via CNED (Centre National d'Enseignement à Distance).

  • Structured Campus Life

    Students do not study at home. They have a clear, daily schedule and attend classes on campus just like traditional students.

  • On-Site Teacher Support

    Our dedicated teachers assist students directly in the classroom, guiding them through the CNED materials and ensuring complete comprehension.

  • Official French Correction

    All major assignments and exams are sent directly to CNED in France for official grading, ensuring absolute academic rigor.

The Language Pipeline

At EFS, language is not just a subject; it is the foundation of global citizenship. We offer intensive language tracks designed to yield official international certifications.

  • The IGCSE English Track

    English instruction begins early. In secondary school, students enter the intensive IGCSE pipeline. They prepare for the IGCSE English as a Second Language exam in 3ème, and advance to the IGCSE English as a First Language exam by Terminale.

  • Spanish Integration

    Official Spanish language classes begin in 6ème (Middle School), though primary students have access to Spanish via extracurricular activities.

The Career & Training Forum

Every year, EFS organizes a major Career and Training Forum. This is a highly anticipated event aimed at helping students discover diverse career paths and align them with their personal aspirations.

The forum brings together professionals from engineering, law, health, finance, and the arts, alongside EFS Alumni and representatives from higher education institutions from France and around the globe.

Parcoursup Integration

Parcoursup is the national platform for admission to the first year of higher education in France. Our pedagogical team provides intensive, individualized support to ensure every student secures their preferred university placement.
