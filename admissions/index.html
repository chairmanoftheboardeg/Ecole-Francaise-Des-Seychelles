Admissions & Pricing

Financial information and tuition breakdown for the 2025/2026 academic year.

Registration Fees

Applicable from September 1st, 2025.

First Registration

15,000 SCR

One-time non-refundable fee for new students.

  • Payable at the latest during registration days.
  • Secures the student's place in the establishment.

Multi-Child Registration Discount

  • 20% discount starting from the 3rd child
  • 40% discount from the 4th child onwards

Pre-Registration

1,000 SCR

Administrative processing fee for new applications.

  • Deducted from the 15,000 SCR First Registration fee if accepted.
  • Non-refundable in case of withdrawal by the parents.
  • Fully refundable if the school cannot offer a place.

Monthly Tuition (Écolages)

Tuition fees are structured by grade level for the 2025-2026 school year.

Academic Level Classes Monthly Rate
Preschool TPS - GS 5,334.00 SCR
Elementary CP - CM2 5,515.00 SCR
Middle School 6ème 7,428.00 SCR
Middle School 5ème - 3ème 7,878.00 SCR
High School 2nde 7,321.00 SCR
High School 1ère 8,059.00 SCR
High School Terminale 9,004.00 SCR

Payment Deadline: Payable in advance, no later than the 25th of each month (e.g., August 25th for September tuition). Any payment made after this date will incur a 10% late penalty.

Tuition Discounts: 2% discount for the 2nd child, 3% for the 3rd, and 4% from the 4th child onwards.

Additional Services & Fees

School Canteen

50 to 65 SCR / Meal

Official Exams

IGCSE exam fees and BAC travel/accommodation costs are paid by the school and rebilled to parents.

IGCSE: ~2,000 SCR
